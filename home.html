Smart Farming

Smart farming is a new idea that refers to farm management that applies the contemporary information and communication technologies to raise the number and quality of crops while minimizing the amount of human work necessary.

  • Sensors: soil, water, light intensity, CO2 Concentration, humidity, temperature, dust
  • Software: new technology software applied for IoT Platform
  • Connection: Wi-Fi, cellular (4G/5G), LoRa
  • Location: GPS, Satellite, ect.
  • Data Record and Analytic: on-Cloud, Local, AI features

Smart Industrial

The Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), which serves as the foundation for Industry 4.0 and Smart Factory, connects smart factories, machines, industrial infrastructure, management systems, and other devices in order to streamline business operations and create intelligent, self-optimizing industrial equipment and facilities. Innovative IoT solutions can handle a variety of difficulties linked to the management of equipment and resources, as well as the security and safety of people.
